# Gas Locations

Gas locations are grouped by pipe group and region.

Initial v0.1 gas location universe:

- HH / Henry Hub
- TETCO-M3
- TETCO-M2
- Transco Z6 NNY
- Transco Z6 NY
- Transco Z5
- Transco Z5 South
- Eastern Gas South / Appalachia

Recognized gas locations with no explicit index family default to GDD.
