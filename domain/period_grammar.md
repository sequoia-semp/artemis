# Period Grammar

Month codes:

```text
F Jan
G Feb
H Mar
J Apr
K May
M Jun
N Jul
Q Aug
U Sep
V Oct
X Nov
Z Dec
```

## Planning year

```text
PY26 = Jun26-May27
```

Planning year is always June-May.

## Gas seasons

```text
X26H27 = Nov26-Mar27
JV27 = Apr27-Oct27
```

XH is gas winter. JV is gas summer.

## Power shorthand

```text
FG26 = Jan26-Feb26
NQ26 = Jul26-Aug26
```

These are standard power strip shorthands and use power reference-hour weighting.

## BALMO

BALMO always excludes the next-day product.
