# Skill: Spread / Basis Decomposition

## Purpose
Decompose quoted spreads into canonical leg exposures.

## Rules
1. FIRST/SECOND means FIRST - SECOND.
2. Signed quantity controls economics.
3. Do not reverse canonical orientation.
4. Power basis uses full LMP.
5. Approved power edges: WH/AD, AD/NI, WH/NI.

## Required output
- QuotedSpread
- first and second leg exposures
- exception for non-canonical orientation
